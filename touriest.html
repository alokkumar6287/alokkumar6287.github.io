Explore the Best Tourist Places in Bihar

!! आइए ना हमरा Bihar में !!

Bodh Gaya

Bodh Gaya is the most famous pilgrimage site in Bihar, where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree.

Bodh Gaya Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Mahabodhi Temple is a must-see for its historical and spiritual significance.

Buddha Stupa

The statue is 18.5 m (61 ft) high representing the Buddha seated in a meditation pose, or dhyana mudra, on a lotus in the open air.

Patna

Patna is the capital of Bihar and is known for its historical and cultural landmarks.

Patna Sahib Gurudwara

This sacred site is one of the most revered Gurudwaras in India, built in honor of Guru Gobind Singh Ji.

Patna Museum

Patna Museum displays a variety of historical artifacts, including fossils, sculptures, and coins from ancient India.

Nalanda

Nalanda was a renowned center of learning and one of the world's oldest universities.

Nalanda University Ruins

The ruins of Nalanda University are a major archaeological site and a symbol of ancient Indian knowledge.

Vaishali

Vaishali is an important historical town associated with Lord Buddha and is known for its ancient ruins and relics.

Vaishali Stupa

The Vaishali Stupa marks the place where Lord Buddha delivered his final sermon and is an important Buddhist monument.

Rajgir

Rajgir is a historical city known for its hot springs, Buddhist monasteries, and scenic beauty.

Vishwa Shanti Stupa

This peace pagoda is a symbol of harmony and was built to commemorate Buddha's message of peace.
